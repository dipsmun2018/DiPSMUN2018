About the committee

"The only possible idea of India is that of a nation greater than the sum of its parts."
- Shashi Tharoor
No country in the world can claim to be as inherently diverse as the Republic of India. Yet, under this diversity, tensions boil rapidly as the citizens are constantly divided by caste, language, and ideology. Vote banks, communal separation, and riots threaten to tear the threads of unity that the idea of this nation is hanging by. To protect this idea, and to resolve the conflicts of the Union, it is important to have a body that makes clear to the various leaders of the various ideologies in this country that their goal should be to build the nation higher, not watch it crumble. The All India Political Party Meet pits political goliaths against underdogs controlling important vote banks. Remember, no matter how high the stakes go, the greatest coalition of all is India herself.
