About the committee

"In the fields the bodies burning, as the war machine keeps turning."
- Black Sabbath, 'War Pigs'
The strangest thing about victory is how temporary it is. Revolutions erupt, are quelled, and erupt again. Leaders change, governments change, but wars never change. Thus, the international community has been pushed to a precipice. Restrict the tools of war, or fall again into the cycle of war. The Disarmament and International Security Committee, or the First Committee, is responsible for peace, security and co-operation of the global community. The world is a tottering table, and the DISEC serves as a balance to stabilize it. Don't rock the table too hard, delegates, or the world is going to come crashing down.
