About the committee

"This is the way the world ends; not with a bang but with a whimper."
- TS Eliot
With the rise of fake news and the impending war between cyber companies and media outlets, there is no committee more powerful or necessary than the Press Corps. Silent, observant, and biased, the International Press Corps shape thoughts, policies, and civilization. In this era, journalists hold the intricacies of power in their pens. Unmasking dictatorships, tearing through societies, discovering stories in the face of ruin - all of these lead to the ultimate goal of every news story: to recover the best obtainable version of the truth from the debris of lies.
