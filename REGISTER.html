Fee structure for the conference -
Individual delegates - ₹1200
Delegations - ₹1200 registration fee + ₹1000 per delegate
(For example, if a delegation of ten persons wishes to attend the conference, then the fees will be ₹1200 + 10×1000 = ₹11200)
Payment for delegations should be made by a demand draft in favour of Delhi Public School Bangalore South. The Draft can be sent by post, can be submitted on the orientation day, or can be handed over on the first day of the MUN.
Individual delegates should pay by cash on the first day of the MUN.
