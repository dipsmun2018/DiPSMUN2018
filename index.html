Youth - The Frontrunners Of Peace And Development

Introduction

Delhi Public School, Bangalore South conducted its first Model United Nations Conference as a small intra school event in 2013. Since then, DiPSMUN has blossomed into one of Bangalore's finest youth conferences, with more and more delegates attending every year. This year, we are back on the 4th, 5th, and 6th of July, and we hope the 2018 edition of DiPSMUN will be much bigger and better. Register now!

Letter from the Secretary General



Delegates,
I invite you to the fifth edition of DiPSMUN with the greatest possible enthusiasm and pride. It is an honour to serve as the Secretary General of DiPSMUN 2018, and to host you at Delhi Public School, Bangalore South, on the 4th, 5th, and 6th of July.

The United Nations is, and always has been the pillar and messenger of peace in a world of war. Seventy three years after its formation, the UN is our most developed and certainly most important international body. The United Nations has always aimed at being a global ‘family’, and aspired to make every member feel protected.

I was part of the organising committee of the very first DiPSMUN, and I have watched the event grow into one of Bangalore’s best youth conferences. I’m confident that every single delegate shall have an enjoyable and empowering experience.

DiPSMUN 2018 is a model UN conference that is going to redefine what MUNs are all about. With nine power packed committees and over four hundred and fifty plus delegates pouring in from every corner of the city, the conference promises, above all else, to deliver at a massive scale.

It is a great platform to understand deeply what actually drives national interests, how superficial or how strong international ties really are, and open your mind to a whole new bunch of world issues - the list is too long. But more importantly, DiPSMUN provides you with an opportunity to test your thinking on your feet, your diplomatic ability, and your leadership skills.

Whether you are declaring war in one of the historic summits, or challenging public policy measures politely in the United Nations Councils, the nine committees of DiPSMUN 2018 have holistic experiences to offer.
I’m excited to present DiPSMUN 2018 before you, and everything it has to offer.

Looking forward to seeing you in July!

Sincerely,
Shikhar Maniar
Secretary General, DiPSMUN 2018
